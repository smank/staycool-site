Privacy Policy

Last updated 18 July 2026

Stay Cool and Stay Cool LLC ("we") runs staycoolandstaycool.com and sells the Cartridge plugin. This policy explains what we collect and why. We keep it minimal: we don't sell your data, and there's no advertising or third-party tracking on this site.

What we collect

  • Beta access email. To reach the beta site you enter an email and receive a one-time PIN. This is handled by Cloudflare Access; we see the email you were invited with.
  • Purchase details. When you buy, checkout and payment are handled by our reseller, Lemon Squeezy, which collects your name, email, and payment information under its own privacy policy. We receive your name, email, and order details — not your full card number.
  • Support email. If you email us, we keep the message and your address to reply and provide support.
  • Watermark data. Purchased and beta builds are stamped with your name and an order/tester ID so a licence can be traced to its buyer. This lives in your copy of the software; we hold the mapping of ID to buyer.

What we don't do

  • No analytics, advertising cookies, or third-party trackers on this site.
  • No selling or renting of your personal data to anyone.
  • The plugin itself has no phone-home, no activation server, and sends us nothing when you use it.

How we use it

To deliver the software and updates, provide support, process your order and refunds, and — for beta builds — identify the source of a leaked copy. That's it.

Processors we rely on

Cloudflare (site hosting and the beta access gate) and Lemon Squeezy (checkout, payment, and download delivery). Each processes data under its own privacy policy.

Your choices

Email us to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you, or to be removed from the beta list. We'll do it.
