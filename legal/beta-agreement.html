Last updated 18 July 2026

Thanks for helping test Cartridge. This is a short, plain-language agreement. By downloading, installing, or using a beta build, you agree to it.

The build is confidential and yours alone

  • The beta build is pre-release and confidential. Don't share it, upload it, post it, or pass on your download link to anyone. One tester, one build.

It's pre-release software

Feedback

Feedback, bug reports, and suggestions you send may be used to improve Cartridge with no obligation or payment to you. You're not required to give feedback, but it's the whole point — so please do.

When the beta ends

When Cartridge is publicly released, or when asked, please delete all beta builds and copies you hold.
