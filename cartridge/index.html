What's in the cartridge

▣

Two engines

Classic: hardware-accurate 2A03 — 2 Pulse, Triangle, Noise, DPCM, plus optional VRC6 expansion (2 Pulse + Sawtooth). Modern: polyphonic with dual oscillators, ADSR, unison, portamento, MPE.

▦

Step sequencer per channel

16 steps × 3 lanes (volume, pitch, duty) on every channel. Tracker-style instrument macros without leaving your DAW. Tempo-synced, loop-capable, recorded into your project.

↻

VGM/VGZ import

Drop a .vgm or .vgz file in. Cartridge parses the 2A03 register stream, detects note boundaries, and turns each instrument into a preset with volume/pitch/duty envelopes intact.

≋

Modern effects chain

BitCrush → Filter (LP/BP/HP) → Chorus → Delay (tempo-syncable) → Reverb. Always visible. Always one knob away. Disabled effects fade so you see what's doing the work.

⌥

Smart MIDI routing

Four routing modes: Split (one MIDI channel per voice), Auto (round-robin with stealing), Mono, Layer. Plays nicely with controllers, sequencers, and DAW MIDI lanes.

⏏

36 factory presets

Curated patches from arcade leads to lo-fi pads, plus the freedom to save and share your own. Step sequence data travels with the preset, including anything imported from VGM.
